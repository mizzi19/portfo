03 : About me

..so this is my story.

All started on the 1st December 1977 in a seaside town of Birzebbuga. In those times it was quite a simple life, school in winter, sea in summer. Scouts and waterpolo were an integtral part of my teens. These two activities were my introduction to team work and competition.

School and work

Secondary school was at the Archbishop's Seminary moving on to St. Aloysius College and finally to University of Malta where I started reading a degree in Engineering. After a year I moved on to 5 years working at the Meteorological office, the last 6 months dedicated to testing a newly acquired system that formed part of a new, modernized office. With money in my pockets, I descovered the joy of travelling. The most mention worthy of all was a month in Kenya and Tanzania with two French friends, ending up climbing Mount Kilimanjaro. That opens another chapter in my life, where I was chosen by Air Malta to form part of an ab-initio program and went to Spain (BAe Flight training in Jerez) to complete my ATPL.
