Python developer

Having a special interest in Data, Machine Learing and Artificial Intelligence, learning Python seemed to be the obvious choice. After some research I decided to follow the Zero to Mastery, 'Complete Python developer in 2020' by Andrei Neagoie. I would not regret this choice as it provided a sound foundation in Python and through multiple projects enhanced knowledge of the different tools and resources available to the Python developer. Andrei's method is easy to follow and teaches by example tackling errors, searching topics and involves the listener as if you are both part of a team working on a particular project. Andrei encourages you to complete the projects by challenging yourself to find the answer and then experiment with customization and research of the topic.
